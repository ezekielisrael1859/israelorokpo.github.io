Client Project
Questionnaire

Thank you for choosing to work with me. This questionnaire is designed to help me understand your business, your vision and your goals deeply — so I can build you a website that truly represents the quality of your brand.

Please answer every question as fully as possible. The more detail you provide, the better I can serve you. There are no wrong answers — I simply want to understand your world.

Please return this completed document within 3-5 working days.

01 Business Information
1. What is the full name of your business?
2. What is your business tagline or motto?
3. How would you describe your business in 2-3 sentences?
4. When was your business founded?
5. Where are you located? (City, State, Country)
6. What are your business hours?
7. What makes your business unique from competitors?
02 Products & Services
8. What products or services do you offer? (list all)
9. Do you offer custom orders? If yes, what kind?
10. Do you offer delivery? If yes, what areas do you cover?
11. What is your price range? (starting prices per product/service)
12. What occasions do you specialize in? e.g. weddings, birthdays, corporate events, naming ceremonies
03 Target Audience
13. Who are your ideal customers? e.g. individuals, corporate clients, event planners, families
14. What age group are your customers mostly?
15. What income level do your customers fall into? Budget / Mid-range / Premium / Luxury
04 Brand Identity
16. Do you have a logo already? Yes / No — If yes, please attach it to this document
17. What colors represent your brand? If you have preferred colors or existing brand colors please list them
18. What feeling do you want your website to give visitors? e.g. warm and homely, luxury and premium, fun and playful, professional and trustworthy
19. Share links to 3 websites you love the look of These help me understand your visual taste
20. Do you have professional photos of your products? Yes / No — If yes, approximately how many?
21. Do you have professional photos of yourself? Yes / No — These are needed for your portfolio/about section
05 Online Presence
22. Instagram handle
23. Facebook page name
24. TikTok handle
25. WhatsApp business number
26. Business email address
06 Website Goals
27. What is the main purpose of the website? e.g. showcase work, take orders, tell brand story, attract corporate clients
28. Do you want customers to order directly on the website or contact you first?
29. Do you want a gallery of your work on the website? Yes / No
30. Do you want customer testimonials on the website? Yes / No — If yes, do you have existing reviews we can use?
31. Are there any specific features you must have on the website? e.g. online payment, booking calendar, price list, WhatsApp chat button
07 Portfolio & Personal Brand
32. What is your full name?
33. How many years have you been baking professionally?
34. What are your biggest achievements or milestones?
35. Have you worked with any notable clients or events?
36. Do you have any certifications or professional training?
37. What do you want potential clients to feel when they see your portfolio?
08 Competitors & Inspiration
38. Who are your main competitors?
39. What do you like about their online presence?
40. What do you NOT want your website to look like?
09 Additional Information
41. Is there anything else you want on the website not covered above?
42. Any other information you think would help me serve you better?
